Recycling in Australia Has Changed.

The recycling industry in Australia has recently been shaken up. Australia has more recycling than ever before, and we need to think of new ways to process tons and tons of reusable waste to make new products.


You Can Help Recycle


Recycling is something that can be done by anyone in Australia. All household recycling can be placed in your household yellow bin. Local councils and local business sometimes offer intakes of different types of products and items. These can all have different lives and be made into many different things.

There Is Quite a Lot


Australia produces a lot of products that can be recycled and made into many different products. Plastics can be remoulded into different applications, and glass bottles and jars can be reused. On average, Australia produces around 30 million tons of recyclables products.

We Have to Deal with All of It


Australia once sent a lot of the collected recycling to different parts of the world to let others deal with our waste. In 2018, China stopped the importing of Australia's recycling. We are now in a time where we need to sort and deal with a lot more recyclable products.
